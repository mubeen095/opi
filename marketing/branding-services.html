Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the difference between marketing and branding?

Marketing promotes your brand while branding defines it. Marketing involves strategies and actions to attract your target audience and sell your product or service. Branding is focused on shaping your brand purpose, vision and message. For instance, if your service branding is about comfort food, the ads you run on social media, discounts and offers are the marketing you do to promote it.
